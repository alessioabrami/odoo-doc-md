# Fatturazione elettronica con Odoo in Brasile¶

Odoo Invoicing is your trusted partner for efficient and legally compliant e-invoicing solutions, tailored to meet Brazil’s regulatory standards. While Odoo covers many key legal cases, it is recommended to verify specific cases with an Odoo advisor to ensure full compliance with the requirements set by the [Secretaria da Fazenda (SEFAZ)](https://www.gov.br/receitafederal/pt-br).

## Quadro normativo della fatturazione elettronica in Brasile¶

In Brazil, e-invoicing is highly regulated and mandatory for most businesses to ensure tax compliance and transparency. The key regulations governing e-invoicing, which is supported through integration with Avalara, include:

  * NF-e: A mandatory e-invoice for goods and services used in B2B transactions.

  * NFS-e: documento rilasciato per le transazioni basate sui servizi, con un’implementazione che varia a seconda del comune.

  * NFC-e: Used for end consumer transactions.

  * Integration with SEFAZ: Odoo connects with Avalara to issue the electronic invoices, which must be authorized by SEFAZ and issued with a unique authorization code (Autorização de Uso).




## Conformità alle normative brasiliane sulla fatturazione elettronica¶

Odoo Fatturazione semplifica il processo di adesione alle normative brasiliane in materia di fatturazione elettronica, offrendo funzionalità progettate per garantire la conformità:

  * **Supported formats** : Odoo supports Brazil’s authorized e-invoice formats, including NF-e for goods and services, NFS-e customizable for municipal requirements, and NFC-e for end consumer transactions. Odoo connects with Avalara to issue these invoices and ensure they are compliant.

  * **Archiviazione e recupero sicuri** : conformemente al requisito di archiviazione quinquennale del Brasile, Odoo offre un’archiviazione inalterabile e un facile recupero in caso di audit o ispezioni fiscali.

  * **Automatic tax calculation** : Odoo automates tax calculations for goods and services, ensuring compliance with regional tax rules.
